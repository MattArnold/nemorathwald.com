Benoit Mandelbrot Has A Posse

Apr. 27, 2007 2:24 PM
nemorathwald
Jonathan Coulton wrote a great song lauding Benoit Mandelbrot and his famous fractal. A discussion on the Penguicon mailing list of inviting Coulton to Penguicon (answer: we really should!) branched into a discussion of what kind of music counts as "filk". Of course Penguicon does not consider such categories when creating the Music track. We only consider what musicians our attendees like, and J.C. is as firmly within that category as anyone on the planet. Anyway, during the filk discussion said: "I consider filk to be music based on a fandom. A song about Something Positive is filk. A song about, say, the Mandlebrot Set is not." What? A denial of Mandelbrot fandom! This cannot be allowed to stand. So I made this as proof.
