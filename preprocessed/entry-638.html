The Way Of The Program

Mar. 9, 2007 10:04 PM
nemorathwald
I just finished reading chapter one of the "Learning With Python" version of "How To Think Like A Computer Scientist". That chapter alone, "The Way of the Program", comprised of a single webpage, is a thing of beauty. I am not just more knowledgeable for having read it; I feel like I am actually smarter. I encourage you to read it. I am considering adapting section 1.8, Formal and Natural Languages, as part of a slide presentation about Lojban geared toward totally non-technical audiences at the next ConFusion.
