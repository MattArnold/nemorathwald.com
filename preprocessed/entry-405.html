A Good Quote

Jan. 6, 2006 5:29 PM
nemorathwald
Paul Z Myers has an entry on his Pharyngula blog concerning some of the world's worst memes. In response to the meme "Abortion stills a beating heart," PZ retorts: "Yeah, and my humanity is embedded entirely in Hodgkin and Huxley's sliding filament theory of muscle contraction, and my mind is just a rhythmic pacemaker potential." His comments to the other memes are similarly wonderful, but this one is my favorite.
