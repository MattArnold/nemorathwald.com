Symphony of Science

Jan. 10, 2010 4:15 PM
nemorathwald
Scientists and philosophers of science are auto-tuned to break out into song, in these music videos of clips from shows such as Cosmos. Featuring the words and voices of Carl Sagan, Stephen Hawking, Neil deGrasse Tyson, Richard Feynman, Bill Nye, Robert Jastrow, Richard Dawkins, and Michio Kaku. "I know that the molecules in my body are traceable To phenomena in the cosmos That makes me want to grab people in the street And say, HAVE YOU HEARD THIS?" - Neil deGrasse Tyson
