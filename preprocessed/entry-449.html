There Is Much Retro Gaming Goodness Lately

Mar. 11, 2006 10:20 PM
nemorathwald
In this video, some guy persecutes his neighborhood in a Link costume accompanied by Legend of Zelda sound effects. No shrubs or flowerpots are safe. I'm still laughing out loud at this. The wonderfulness overwhelms me. "YOU GOT PANTS!" The classic games Metroid and Ghosts 'n Goblins have been hacked to replace the main characters with the equally-classic hero Megaman. Yes, if you played Megaman as a kid you guessed it: when you defeat Arthur you can use his spear, and when you defeat Samus Aran you get to use her missile. Three great eight-bit tastes that taste great together for your nostalgic pleasure. It reminds you that masterful game design and execution is never obsolete. They're playable online: Megaman vs Ghosts 'n Goblins and Megaman vs Metroid.
