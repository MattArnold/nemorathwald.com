Crib Notes For The Turing Test

Mar. 24, 2005 5:14 PM
nemorathwald
Zompist.com has a humorous set of notes that an artificial intelligence might need in order to pass the Turing Test. For example: Clever questioners will ask about your feelings. Safe responses: 1. I'm anxious. 2. I'm depressed. 3. I'm hungry. Bad responses: 1. I'm itchy. 2. I'm righteously indignant. 3. Constipation's gone!
