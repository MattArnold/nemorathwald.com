My fan art

Feb. 9, 2005 11:50 PM

Lots of people do fan art. I do fan art of Penguicon. Don't expect to see this art on the new website, or for that matter, to see it ever again. But it took a long time and I want somebody to see it. The following art is personal and totally unofficial. Do not interpret anything about it as a statement about Penguicon looking like a flyer for a rave party, or as a statement about your favorite operating system. Credit is to to the transhumanist artist and collaborative SF author from Sweden, Anders Sandberg, for the fractal in the background of this image.
